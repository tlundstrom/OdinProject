Black Cypress Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz Yeast
  • 1.5 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1.75 Cups Milk
  • 6oz Butter
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2oz Salt
  • 3lb 9.6oz AP Flour

Procedure

  1. Dissolve the sugar into the milk and cream and bring the temerature between 95F and 110F. Do not overheat it will kill the yeast.
  2. Measure the yeast out into a bowl of a large stand mixer and pour over the warm milk.
  3. Cover this with the flour and poke a small hole in the flour to monitor the yeasts activity.
  4. Add the salt into a similar indentation, keeping it separated so it doesnt affect the yeast too early.
  5. Melt the butter but keep it emulsified if possible and within the temperature range that the yeast needs.
  6. When the yeast is ready and quite active add the eggs and butter.
  7. Mix the dough on medium-low speed for 5 minutes
  8. Wrap the bowl with plastic wrap or a damp cloth and allow to proof until doubled in size, roughly one hour.
  9. Gently roll the dough into 2oz balls until smooth on top.
  10. Let rise again until doubled in size
  11. Bake at 375F for 10 minutes, rotating halfway through.
