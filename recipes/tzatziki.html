Tzatziki

Ingredients

  • 908g Greez Yogurt
  • 1134g Sour Cream
  • 250g Cucumbers
  • 37.5g Garlic
  • 15g Dill
  • 37.5g Lemon Juice
  • 18g White Whine Vinegar
  • 20g Salt

Procedure

  1. Grate cucumbers over linen, strain and squeezeout as much juice as possible
  2. Chop the dill and microplane the garlic.
  3. Combine the cucumber solids, lemon, vinegar, salt, dill and garlic.
  4. Add the sour cream and mix to combine.
  5. Fold in the yogurt and season to taste. Do not overmix at this stage. End result should be thick.
