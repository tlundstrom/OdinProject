Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 5oz Egg yolks
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Marsala
  • 2 Lbs Marscarpone
  • 2 Cups Heavy Cream, Whipped
  • 1.5 Quarts Double Strong Coffee
  • 108-117 Lady Fingers
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Shaved Chocolate

Procedure

  1. Mix yolks, sugar and marsala in a steel mixing bowl.
  2. Place above a boiling water bath and stir constantly until froth dissapears and mixture stats to slowly bubble.
  3. Agitate marscarpone to loosen it up.
  4. Whisk into egg mixture in two additions.
  5. Fold in whipped cream and let sit in refigerator for 10-15 minutes before use.
  6. Dip 3 lady fingers in coffee and place in a deep cappuccino cup
  7. Cover witha single layer of cooled cream mixture and sprinkle liberally with cocoa powder
  8. Repeat two more times in cup.
  9. Recipe makes 12 to 13 cups.
  10. Top with shaved chocolate
